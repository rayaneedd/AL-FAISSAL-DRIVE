Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You need a valid driver's license, a passport or ID card, and a credit card in the driver's name for the deposit.

Are there mileage limits?

We offer unlimited mileage on all our rentals so you can explore Morocco freely.

Can I pick up the car at Agadir Airport?

Yes, we offer free delivery and drop-off at Agadir Al Massira Airport for your convenience.
