Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you rent.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You need a valid driver's license (held for at least 1 year), a passport or national ID card, and a credit card in the driver's name for the security deposit.

Are there mileage limits?

No! We offer unlimited mileage on all our rentals so you can explore Morocco freely without worrying about extra fees.

Can I pick up the car at Agadir Airport?

Yes, we offer free delivery and drop-off at Agadir Al Massira Airport for your convenience. Just provide your flight details when booking.

What type of insurance is included?

Standard comprehensive insurance with a deductible is included in all our rates. You can upgrade to full coverage (zero deductible) at the counter.
